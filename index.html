The Rules

This is a very simple game. The aim is to lift two cards at a time, and match them.

To play, just click on two cards and if they match, you have won those. If not, just try again. The challenge is to remember the positioning of each card that you don't win so you that can match them when you pick its companion up

Once you have matched all the cards, you have won the game!
